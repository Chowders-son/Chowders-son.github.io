About Me

I’m Nsika Dludlu, a Computer Science professional specializing in data science, set to graduate from the College of Idaho in December 2024. I’ve gained experience as a Web Developer, optimizing websites and managing operational systems, and as a Database Developer at Exor Petroleum, creating scalable SQL databases to streamline fuel distribution processes. Additionally, I’ve freelanced on projects like building an open data repository and exploring predictive modeling for large language models. Skilled in Python, JavaScript, SQL, and AWS, I’m passionate about solving real-world problems with technology and eager to contribute to innovative, impactful projects.
